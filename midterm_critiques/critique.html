A World of Terror

The data visualization example I pick is ‘A World of Terror’ created by. This interactive visualization integrates all the information of the killed, the wounded and the number of the terrorist incidents every year from 1970 to 2013. Categorized into different terrorist groups, the graph precisely shows the historic activity changes of each terrorist group in each month as well as the impact of the year. Also, the tabs on the top can switch the ranking of the groups according to different criteria, which provides readers with various ways to explore the data themselves.
With the number of floors as the x-axis and the built year as the y-axis, this diagram shows the trace of height changes of the buildings in Manhattan from 1765 to 2017. Each dot in the diagram represents one building in Manhattan. Over the last 250 years, the height of the buildings in Manhattan has skyrocketed. The number of floors has raised from 3-4 floors to more than 100.
